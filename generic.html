About me

I am a Web Developer in New York, looking for opportunities.

I specialize in Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, and React.js, and recently made the projects that you can see here. Before I became a software developer, I studied Finance. While doing so, I found myself often writing simple Python scripts to help me complete my work. It wasn't long before I realized that I was actually having more fun writing the scripts than actually thinking about Finance.

After I graduated, I started considering coding more seriously. I've always been interested in code, but I took only a few coding related courses in school. So, I ended teaching myself a little bit of Ruby and applying to App Academy.

Over the months that followed at App Academy, I learned a slew of languages and frameworks, starting from Ruby, and moving through SQL, Rails, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Flux. Now I'm ready to further expand my horizons as a developer through a career.
